Insalata Caprese II

Because this salad is so simple, fresh, top-quality tomatoes and mozzarella are important.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • â…“ cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • fine sea salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste

    • Step

    1. On a large platter, alternate and overlap the tomato slices, mozzarella cheese slices, and basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil.
    2. Season with sea salt and pepper.
